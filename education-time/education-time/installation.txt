Installation.

This ZIP-archive contains two folders.

education-time - must be placed in the template-folder of your PivotX-installation.
education-time-slides - must be placed in the images-folder of your PivotX-installation.

If you want to change the images, texts or links in the gallery, you must edit the file _sub_gallery.tpl.
