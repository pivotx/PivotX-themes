Licensed under the MIT License: http://www.opensource.org/licenses/mit-license.php

To replace the images and the accompagnying text with your own, you must edit the 
file: _sub_slideshow.tpl

The images are placed in the images folder inside the template folder, but you
can place them where ever you like, if you adjust _sub_slideshow.tpl accordingly.
